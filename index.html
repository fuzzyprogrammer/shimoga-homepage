Shimoga

The Gateway of Malnad

Shimoga, also known as Shivamogga, is a city in Karnataka, India, located on the banks of the Tunga River. It is popularly nicknamed the “Gateway of Malnad” due to its proximity to the Western Ghats

A Rich Historical Tapestry

Shimoga has a rich history, having been ruled by various dynasties such as the Kadambas, Chalukyas, Hoysalas, and the Vijayanagara rulers. It was also visited by Mahatma Gandhi during the Satyagraha movement

Shimoga: A Smart City in the Making

Selected for the Smart Cities project, Shimoga is undergoing significant development to enhance its infrastructure and services, aiming to become a model smart city in Karnataka.

Center for Education

Shimoga is an important center for high school and pre-university education in Karnataka, with notable institutions like Jnanadeepa School and Sri Aurobindo PU College
